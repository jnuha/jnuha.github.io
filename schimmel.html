Why not let gross stuff grow on colored pencils?

For the course industrial design in the second semester, we were asked to destroy something and document the process. I shaved off a white colored pencil and placed the bits into a glass bottle with water. I put in some food bits that went bad, and left the bottle warm for a few days. Afterwards, what I got was a mush of wood, pencil lead and fungus.
