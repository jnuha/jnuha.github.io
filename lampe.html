Using a chocolate bar to package light.

This is a DIY project where I used a chocolate packaging and a rubber band to create a lamp shade. I cut up the packaging in a pattern to create interesting flaps and textures. Depending on how much the flaps are pushed in or out, different lighting effects are created. I found that the chocolate packaging in itself creates intriguing geometries at the fringes, which adds to the visual variety of the textures.
