Humble Table

CONTACT ABOUT ME MY WORK

Because there are people who like to sit on the floor.

In the Korean culture, it is common for people to sit on the floor for eating, talking etc. While playing around with a washbag that I took apart, I decided to create a height-adjustable table whose height can be adjusted depending on how the acryl panels making up the table are organised. The panels have slim holes in order to reduce weight for better portability. I call the table "Humble Table" because it can stoop surprisingly low, which allows the table to be used in various scenarios - at a picnic, balcony, indoors... and with my family, who enjoys sitting on the floor from time to time. ðŸ™‚
