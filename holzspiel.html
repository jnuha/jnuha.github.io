Cubes, dice, colors and players, and the rest is up to your imagination.

For the course interaction design in the first semester, we made a wood game. We built a three-stage playing board and filled it with wooden cubes. Each wooden cube has a color. Players place their figures at the starting point at the bottom and take a turn to roll the dice. The players proceed according to the dice's number. They flip the cube on which they landed to check its color. The color means something different depending on the "adventure" story that the players choose.
