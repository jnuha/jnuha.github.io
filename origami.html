From a surface to a morphed body in tension.

For the course Gestaltungsgrundlagen in the first semester, we were given the theme "active-passive" to work with. Based on my sketchbook theme "folding (Falten)" and "overlapping (√úberlagerung)", I experimented with foldings, folding printed images of foldings, boxes, strings with magnets, plastic and paper (details in the documentation). My end result is a patterned, colored paper that lies still with a string (passive) until the observer interacts with it to bring diverse forms into action (active) and holds it in tension with the string.
